<h1>SolarNight</h1>
<p>Hi! You’re a wickedly powerful <em><strong><span style="color: #ff00ff;">WIZARD</span></strong></em> who has stolen... the <em><strong><span style="color: yellow;">SUN</span></strong></em>. But, much to everyone’s surprise, people aren’t too pleased about it&hellip; So the <em><strong><span style="color: white;">KING</span></strong></em> of the whole <em><strong><span style="color: white;">WORLD</span></strong></em> has decided to take it back from you!&nbsp;</p>
<p><br>Can <em><strong><span style="color: white;">YOU</span></strong></em> protect the sun and stand against an entire fleet sent after you? Let’s find out!</p>
<p><img src="https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI2NTMzMTMzLmdpZg==/original/HWr2Q3.gif"><br></p>
<h1>How to play?&nbsp;</h1>
<p>Enemy ships will advance on you with each wave. Using <em><strong><span style="color: brown;">MATERIALS</span></strong></em>, you can build <em><strong><span style="color: GREY;">CANNONS</span></strong></em> to defend your lighthouse!<br><img src="https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI2NTMzNTA5LmdpZg==/original/EiKPGX.gif"><br></p>
<p>For your cannons to work, they must be powered by <em><strong><span style="color: yellow;">LIGHT</span></strong></em> from the lighthouse. <em><strong><span style="color: #0080ff;">MIRRORS</span></strong></em> will help you direct the light to where it’s needed.<em><em><br><img src="https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI2NTMzODQzLmdpZg==/original/PUXwGK.gif"><br></em></em></p>
<p>To strengthen your cannons, direct <em><strong><span style="color: white;">LIGHT FROM LIGHTHOUSE</span></strong></em> onto them.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI2NTM0MTQ3LmdpZg==/original/ZcVzX3.gif"><br></p>
